About me

Hey there, I'm Marks Privalovs, a 26-year-old dream-chaser born back in '98 in the charming town of Jurmala, Latvia. My journey is all about rolling with the punches and bouncing back stronger. Picture this: I was knee-deep in soccer from age 6, but a pesky injury at 16 threw a curveball my way. So, I switched gears, finding comfort in other cool stuff like diving into computer games.

Fast forward to when I hit the ripe age of 20—I decided to shake things up and landed in Moscow, Russia, where I took on the role of a video filming administrator. Let me tell you, it was a wild ride filled with crazy experiences, meeting awesome folks, and crafting digital magic that you can now spot all over the web. But here's the cool part: Moscow wasn't just about scoring cool jobs; it opened my eyes to the fascinating worlds of blockchain, crypto, and programming. I dove headfirst into these passions, kicking off my crypto journey in 2021 and soaking up over two years of knowledge.

Then, in 2023, at the fresh age of 24, I boomeranged back to Latvia. Why? To dive into programming studies and turn those newfound dreams into reality. Now, I'm on a mission—picture me as a future web developer and crypto enthusiast, fueled by a never-ending quest for growth and grit.
